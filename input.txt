Recipe: Meatball

Ingredients:
- 1 kg of beef
- 2 kg of pork
- 4.25 g of oregano
- 1 L of milk
- 1 g of breadcrumbs
- 2 pieces of bowl

Directions:
1. Add oregano to beef then add beef to pork.
2. Mix milk and beef into mixture.
3. If mixture is as much as breadcrumbs, add pork to beef.
4. While mixture is not done, keep baking mixture.
5. Serve mixture.
