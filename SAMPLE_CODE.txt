Recipe: Meatball

Ingredients:
- 65 kg of beef (int)
- 10 kg of pork (int)
- 4.25 g of oregano (double)
- 66 L of milk (int)
- 1 g of breadcrumbs (double)
- 1 g of mixture (double)
- 67 L of water (int)

Directions:
1. Add oregano to beef then add beef to pork. (Two add statements)
2. Mix milk and beef into mixture. (Mix is multiplication)
3. If mixture is as much as breadcrumbs, remove pork from beef, 
otherwise add breadcrumbs to mixture. (If-else statement)
4. While mixture is not done, keep baking mixture. (While statement, baking is incrementing)
5. Chill water. (decrement)
6. Check oregano, pork, and beef. (print)
7. Weight beef, milk, water, and pork. (print as char)
