Recipe: Bread

Ingredients:
- 600 mL of flour
- 600 mL of water
- 10 kg of starter
- 1 kg of yeast
- 84 kg of nuts
- 70 kg of berries
- 30 kg of cheese
- 15 kg of pho
- 40 g of pudding

Directions:
1. If water is more than flour, weight nuts, otherwise weight berries.
2. Weight starter.
3. If water is more than or equal to flour and yeast is done, weight nuts, otherwise weight berries.
4. Weight starter.
5. Bake yeast then check yeast. (increment)
6. Chill flour for 4 mins then check flour.
7. While starter is not done, keep chilling starter then check starter.
8. Repeatedly bake starter then check starter until starter is as much as berries.
9. Remove starter and flour from yeast then check yeast. (int = int - int - float)
10. Remove flour and starter from yeast then check yeast. (int = int - float - int)
11. Remove yeast and starter from flour then check flour. (float = float - int - int)
12. Remove starter and water from flour then check flour. (float = float - int - float)
13. Add cheese and pudding to cheese then check cheese. (int = int + float + int)
13. Add pudding and cheese to cheese then check cheese. (int = float + int + int)
14. Add cheese and pho to water then check water. (float = int+int+float)
15. Add water and flour and cheese to pudding then check pudding. (float = float+float+int+float)
