Recipe: Bread

Ingredients:
- 600 mL of flour
- 600 mL of water
- 10 kg of starter
- 1 kg of yeast
- 84 kg of nuts
- 70 kg of berries

Directions:
1. If water is more than flour, weight nuts, otherwise weight berries.
2. Weight starter.
3. If water is more than or equal to flour and yeast is done, weight nuts, otherwise weight berries.
4. Weight starter.
5. Bake yeast then check yeast.
6. Bake flour for 4 mins then check flour.
